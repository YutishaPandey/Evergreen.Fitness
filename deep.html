Deep Breathing Meditation

Purpose:

To relax and calm your mind by focusing on your breath.

Instructions:

  1. Find a Comfortable Position: Sit or lie down in a comfortable position with your back straight.
  2. Close Your Eyes: Gently close your eyes to help focus inward.
  3. Focus on Your Breath: Take a deep breath in through your nose, counting to four as you inhale. Hold your breath for a count of four.
  4. Exhale Slowly: Breathe out through your mouth for a count of six. Feel the air leaving your body.
  5. Repeat: Continue this deep breathing for 5-10 minutes, focusing on the rise and fall of your chest with each breath.
