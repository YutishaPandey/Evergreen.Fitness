Seated Twist

Description:
Start Position: Sit on the edge of a chair with your feet flat on the floor and your back straight.
Twist: Place your right hand on the back of the chair and gently twist your torso to the right, looking over your right shoulder.
Hold: Hold the twist for 5 seconds, then return to the center.
Switch Sides: Repeat the twist on the left side.

Benefits:
Improves Spine Flexibility: Enhances flexibility in the spine and upper back.
Aids Digestion: Stimulates digestive organs and helps with digestion.
Increases Mobility: Helps maintain and improve range of motion.

Time Left: 45
