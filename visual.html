Guided Visualization

Purpose:

To create a peaceful mental image to reduce stress.

Instructions:

  1. Sit Comfortably: Sit or lie down in a relaxed position. Close your eyes.
  2. Imagine a Place: Picture a peaceful place in your mind. It could be a beach, a forest, or any place you find calming.
  3. Engage Your Senses: Imagine what you see, hear, smell, and feel in this place. For example, you might see waves, hear birds, smell flowers, and feel the warmth of the sun.
  4. Relax: Spend 5-10 minutes immersed in this mental image, letting yourself feel calm and relaxed.
