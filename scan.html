Body Scan Meditation

Purpose:

To increase body awareness and release tension.

Instructions:

  1. Get Comfortable: Sit or lie down comfortably. Close your eyes if you’re comfortable doing so.
  2. Focus on Your Toes: Start by bringing your attention to your toes. Notice any sensations or tension.
  3. Move Up the Body: Slowly move your focus up through your feet, legs, abdomen, chest, arms, neck, and head. Spend a few seconds on each area, noticing how it feels.
  4. Release Tension: If you find areas of tension, take a deep breath and imagine relaxing those muscles.
  5. Finish: Once you’ve scanned the entire body, take a few deep breaths and slowly open your eyes.
