Veggie Omelette

Preparation:

Prepare the Ingredients:
Chop spinach, tomatoes, mushrooms, and onions.

Cook the Vegetables:
In a non-stick skillet, saut√© the onions and mushrooms in a little oil or butter until soft.
Add tomatoes and spinach and cook until the spinach is wilted.

Prepare the Eggs:
Beat eggs or egg whites in a bowl.

Combine:
Pour the beaten eggs over the cooked vegetables in the skillet.
Cook until the eggs are set, then fold the omelette in half.

Serve:
Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve warm.

Health Benefits:

  • Nutrient-Rich: The vegetables in the omelette provide essential vitamins and minerals, such as vitamins A and C, which support overall health and immune function.
  • Protein: Eggs are a great source of high-quality protein, which is important for muscle repair and growth.
  • Low in Carbs: The omelette is low in carbohydrates, making it a good option for maintaining stable blood sugar levels.
  • Digestive Health: Vegetables add fiber to the diet, which supports healthy digestion.
  • Brain Health: Eggs contain choline, which is beneficial for brain health and cognitive function.
