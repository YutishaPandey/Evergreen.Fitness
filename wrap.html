Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Instructions:

Prepare the Ingredients:
Slice the avocado and tomato.
Wash and dry the lettuce.

Assemble the Wrap:
Lay a whole grain tortilla flat on a clean surface.
Spread a layer of sliced turkey breast evenly across the tortilla.
Add avocado slices, lettuce, and tomato on top of the turkey.

Wrap and Serve:
Roll the tortilla tightly to enclose the fillings.
Slice the wrap in half if desired and serve immediately.

Health Benefits:

  • Lean Protein: Sliced turkey breast is a good source of lean protein, which supports muscle health and satiety.
  • Whole Grains: The whole grain tortilla provides fiber and essential nutrients, contributing to better digestion and sustained energy.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado provides heart-healthy fats and contributes to overall nutritional balance.
  • Nutrient-Rich: Lettuce and tomato add vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, supporting immune health and hydration.
  • Balanced Meal: This wrap is a balanced meal with a good mix of protein, fiber, healthy fats, and essential nutrients, making it ideal for maintaining energy levels throughout the day.
