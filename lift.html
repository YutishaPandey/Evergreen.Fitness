Seated Leg Lifts

Description:
Start Position: Sit on the edge of a chair with your back straight and hands resting on your thighs.
Lift Leg: Slowly extend your right leg out straight, keeping your foot flexed. Hold for a few seconds.
Lower Leg: Gently lower your leg back down. Repeat with the left leg.

Benefits:
Strengthens Legs: Builds strength in the thigh and hip muscles.
Improves Circulation: Helps to boost blood flow in the legs.
Enhances Balance: Supports overall balance and stability.

Time Left: 45
