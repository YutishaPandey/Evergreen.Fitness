Oatmeal with Berries and Nuts

Preparation:

Prepare the Oats:
In a pot, combine 1 cup of oats with 2 cups of water or milk.
Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Add Berries:
Once the oats are cooked, stir in a handful of blueberries or strawberries.

Add Nuts:
Sprinkle in some walnuts or almonds.

Season:
Add a dash of cinnamon to taste.

Serve:
Spoon the oatmeal into bowls and serve warm.

Health Benefits:

  • Heart Health: Oats are rich in soluble fiber, which can help lower cholesterol levels and improve heart health.
  • Antioxidants: Berries are high in antioxidants, which can help protect cells from damage and reduce inflammation.
  • Healthy Fats: Nuts provide healthy fats and protein, which are good for brain health and maintaining energy levels.
  • Digestive Health: The fiber in oats aids in digestion and can help prevent constipation.
  • Blood Sugar Control: Oats have a low glycemic index, which helps stabilize blood sugar levels.
