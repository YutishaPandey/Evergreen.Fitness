Greek Yogurt

Preparation:

Prepare the Yogurt:
Spoon Greek yogurt into a bowl or glass.

Add Berries:
Layer mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries) on top of the yogurt.

Add Granola:
Sprinkle a bit of granola over the berries.

Drizzle Honey:
Drizzle a small amount of honey on top.

Serve:
Serve immediately or chill until ready to eat.

Health Benefits:

  • Protein: Greek yogurt is high in protein, which supports muscle health and keeps you full longer.
  • Calcium: Greek yogurt is a good source of calcium, which is important for bone health.
  • Antioxidants and Vitamins: Mixed berries provide vitamins and antioxidants that boost the immune system and overall health.
  • Digestive Health: The probiotics in Greek yogurt promote a healthy gut flora and improve digestion.
  • Energy Boost: Granola adds additional fiber and energy to the meal, keeping you energized throughout the morning.
