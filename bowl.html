Quinoa and Veggie Bowl

Instructions:

Cook the Quinoa:
Rinse 1 cup of quinoa under cold water.
In a pot, combine the rinsed quinoa with 2 cups of water.
Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.

Roast the Vegetables:
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
Chop bell peppers, zucchini, and carrots into bite-sized pieces.
Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly browned.

Assemble the Bowl:
In a bowl, place a serving of cooked quinoa.
Top with roasted vegetables.
Add avocado slices and a dollop of hummus.

Serve:
Mix together and enjoy!

Health Benefits:

  • Complete Protein: Quinoa is a complete protein, containing all nine essential amino acids, which is beneficial for muscle health and repair.
  • Nutrient-Dense: Roasted vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, supporting overall health and reducing inflammation.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado provides healthy fats, which are good for heart health and maintaining energy levels.
  • Digestive Health: The fiber in quinoa and vegetables aids in digestion and promotes gut health.
  • Hummus Benefits: Hummus adds protein and fiber, and provides healthy fats from chickpeas and tahini.
